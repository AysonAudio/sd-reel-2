Project Highlights

  • Logo Sound Design

Logo Sound Design
A stinger for the logo intro. I created it using: - An orchestral snare roll VST. - An orchestral cymbal crash VST. - Some sound effects libraries.
Ambience Sound Design
A snippet of the very soft ambience I designed to complement the music. I designed it so that: - The ambience changes every time the film cuts to a new shot. - Far-away camera shots sound sparser and less active. - Close-up camera shots sound fuller and more active.
